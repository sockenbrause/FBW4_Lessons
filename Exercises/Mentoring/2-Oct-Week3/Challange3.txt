Remove Vowels from a string :
Write a regular expression that removes all vowels from a string. 

Examples
removeVowels("candy") ➞ "cndy"

removeVowels("hello") ➞ "hll"
//Note : The "e" is removed, but also the "o" is removed

removeVowels("apple") ➞ "ppl"

Notes

removeVowels actually does remove all vowels.
